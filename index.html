Repurposing Wind Turbine Blades of the New York City Wind Farms

Explore Our Team Capstone Project

New York state has been increasingly siting wind farms across the state and most recently has approved sites closer to the New York City area, as part of its expanding renewable energy strategy. Wind turbine blades are often decommissioned before the end of usefule life due to material and other technological advance to increase efficieny.
Decommisioned blades need to go somewhere; either they are recovered and reused in other applications, or they are landfilled.
This project will: Assess the future of recovery and direct reuse of decommisioned turbine blades in New York to keep them out of landfills Develop a plan to support future reuse of decommisioned wind turbine blades Design a geographical model to minimize GHG emissions for the reuse plan.
